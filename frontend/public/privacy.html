Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm reservations.
  • To communicate with you about bookings or inquiries.
  • To improve our website and customer experience.

3. Data Protection

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at info@skyapartments.com.
